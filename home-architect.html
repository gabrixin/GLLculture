THE CONCEPT

These handpainted pieces took form from the signature vision of the author representing the freedom of expression and the connection between art, music and the higher self. The artistic perspective is most often triggered by music. With time, the influence of sounds has objectified into a language which transforms sounds into shapes, therefore creating a codependent relationship between music and art.

GLL is a creative lifestyle brand. Our wearable artworks represent your higher self and the intersection of art and music culture. We believe in the power of creativity to change lives. Our vision is to create a community of people who celebrate their individuality and connect through creativity. We want GLL to be the go-to destination for anyone who wants to express themselves in a meaningful way. Whether you’re an artist, musician, or just someone who appreciates creativity, we want GLL to be the place where you can find your voice.

MEET THE ARTIST

GL.S.- Visual Artist

"I don't want to be known by name, I wanna bring people together through my art. I wanna start an art movement"

I'm painting my dreams into reality. I always saw them trying to say something about themselves through fashion. I started researching and giving the people what they wanted: a way to express themselves freely. Music and art are codependent. I used music to create art. One of my biggest achievements in life is seeing artists, party people, DJs, singers, and painters wear my artwork because they resonate with the concept of showcasing their dramatic personality through fashion.

When I paint, I often feel a chaotic mix of emotions. The sensation of my heart beating faster is caused by both excitement and fear – the anticipation of creating something new that may not turn out the way I imagined it. The act of painting itself also causes my emotions to become heightened; allowing me to tap into feelings that may have otherwise been buried beneath the surface. This can lead to tears as I find myself overwhelmed with emotion – especially when my artistic vision gets triggered by music. For me, this chaotic mix of emotions is essential to my creativity. It gives me permission to express myself freely and take risks in seeking out something unique, rather than simply copying what has already been done. My artwork benefits from being unencumbered by expectations or boundaries; giving me the freedom to explore and create something truly special and meaningful.
